Data Protection Statement

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 26 June 2026

1. Overview of Data Collection

This website serves exclusively as a static information and distribution platform for the digital edition of Perfect Answers, Perfect Questions. It is designed to prioritize visitor anonymity and does not collect, track, or process personal data.

2. Personal Information & Tracking

We do not utilize user registration systems, contact forms, comment sections, or newsletter sign-ups. No cookies are stored on your device, and no automated user-profiling tools or third-party analytics trackers are active on this site.

3. Hosting & Server Logs

Because this platform is deployed using modern static hosting providers (such as GitHub Pages), certain technical infrastructure data, such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamp, may be automatically processed in standard server access logs to guarantee connection stability and operational infrastructure security. This server-level processing is managed externally by the host and is not accessible to or retained by the author for identifying individual users.

4. Digital Downloads

Interacting with the digital distribution gateway on the download page confirms your voluntary acknowledgment of the digital reading agreement. Accepting this checkbox state is processed purely via client-side scripts inside your local browser instance and does not transmit notifications or telemetry data back to our servers.

5. External Links

This platform may reference external web domains for reference purposes or verification badges. Once you leave this server footprint by tracking an external hyperlink anchor, your browsing interactions are governed by the specific privacy policies of those external domains.
