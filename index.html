About the Book

Rather than asking readers to reject belief, this book asks whether belief itself becomes stronger when it survives honest examination.

About the Author

Sourav Uttarkabat spent several years immersed in Krishna Consciousness, studying Gaudiya Vaishnava theology, living within temple communities, and defending the philosophy with conviction.

Over time, that conviction gave way to investigation as questions accumulated across philosophy, psychology, history, archaeology, biology, and institutional behavior.

This book is the result of that journey—not to replace one certainty with another, but to encourage investigation guided by evidence, intellectual honesty, and the willingness to revise one's conclusions.
